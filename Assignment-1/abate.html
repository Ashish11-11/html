abate

  • Word Meanings of abate

    • to decrease in force or intensity.
      • the storm suddenly abated.
    • to decrease in amount or value.
      • The legacies abated proportionately.
    • reduce or remove (a nuisance).
      • this action would not have been sufficient to abate the odour nuisance.
